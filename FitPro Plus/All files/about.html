About FitPlan Pro

Welcome to FitPlan Pro, a personal project by Saad Salman Beg.

As a Computer Science student at the University of Houston, I have combined my passion for fitness and technology to create FitPlan Pro. This platform is designed to provide personalized workout plans that cater to your unique fitness goals, whether you're aiming for muscle gain, weight loss, endurance, flexibility, or general fitness.

FitPlan Pro is an ongoing project, reflecting my commitment to continuous improvement and innovation. I am dedicated to enhancing the user experience and expanding the features available on this platform over time.

Thank you for visiting FitPlan Pro. I hope this platform helps you on your fitness journey. Your feedback and support are greatly appreciated as I continue to develop and improve FitPlan Pro.
